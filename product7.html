Large Ponytail Palm Bonsai also known as Beaucarnea recurvata is a species of perennial plant native to Mexico. It is known for its distinctive, swollen base, which can resemble a ponytail, and its long thin leaves. These trees thrive in low light conditions; and require infrequent watering and feeding.
