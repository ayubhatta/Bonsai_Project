The Cotoneaster family is composed of various plants, including small trees, evergreen shrubs, and deciduous shrubs. Some of these are commonly used as bonsai trees due to their small leaves and dense branching. The fruits of some of these species, such as the horizontalis, are additionally attractive and can add a bit of interest to the overall design.

A cotoneaster bonsai tree needs to be well-drained and placed in partial shade or full sun. It should also be regularly watered and pruned back to maintain its shape. These trees can tolerate mild winter but should be kept indoors during the winter.
