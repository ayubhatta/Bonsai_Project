The ideal temperature range for Grand Golden Gate ficus bonsai trees is between 60 and 85 degrees Fahrenheit. They prefer high humidity, thus it's advisable to maintain a humidity tray or frequently water the leaves. To keep the soil moist but not soggy, it also needs well-draining soil, regular watering, and fertilizer.

These bonsai plants also need to be pruned and shaped to keep their shape, although caution should be used to avoid over-pruning or harming the tree's fragile branches. Grand Golden Gate Ficus are susceptible to environmental changes, and they can lose their leaves if they are moved around excessively or are subjected to sharp fluctuations in humidity or temperature.
