A popular bonsai tree is the miniature ginseng ficus (Ficus microcarpa), a kind of fig tree. It originates from China, India, and Southeast Asia and is also known as "Chinese banyan" or "Malayan banyan." It is a little evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves and aerial roots that give it a twisted, distinctive look.

Because they are very simple to maintain and can be grown indoors, miniature ginseng ficus bonsai are a fantastic choice for beginners or intermediate bonsai enthusiasts as well as those who reside in colder locations. It can live for many years and develop into a lovely and one-of-a-kind miniature tree with the right care and attention.
