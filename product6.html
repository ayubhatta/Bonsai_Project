Large Ginkgo Tree

$68.00
$54.40

Large Ginkgo Tree is indigenous to China and is considered a living fossil as it dates to prehistory. The leaves of this tree are two-lobed and fan-shaped, making them unique and distinctive. In the fall, they turn a beautiful golden color which is short lived as once leaf drop starts to occur, it will be finished in a matter of days.

An outdoor deciduous tree that requires a dormant period, this bonsai requires winter protection in colder climates, it'll also thrive in full sun to partial shade.
