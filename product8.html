A trained Juniper bonsai is a tiny version of a Juniper tree that has undergone the bonsai growing and shaping procedure. In order to give the tree the look of a mature, full-size tree in nature, this process includes pruning, wiring, and styling the tree.

It is a traditional bonsai tree, with a natural, rugged appearance and a distinctive shape. It should be fertilized and watered regularly and pruned to maintain its shape.
